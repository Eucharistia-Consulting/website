Technical Due Diligence for Search Funds & Private Equity

Is the software you are buying an asset that will scale, or a liability that will stall growth?

Search Funds often target stable, profitable companies running on legacy stacks like PHP, Java, and .NET. On the surface, the product works. Underneath, it may be riddled with technical debt, security vulnerabilities, or architectural "spaghetti code" that makes modernization impossible.

We provide the forensic insight investors need to de-risk acquisitions of legacy software companies.

The "Red Flag" Report

We specialize in fixed-fee, high-impact technical audits for investors under LOI. We move beyond standard automated scans to perform deep architectural sampling of the codebase.

We answer three critical questions before you close:

  • Will it scale? Identification of architectural bottlenecks (e.g., database-in-loop, local file dependencies) that will break under your growth plan.
  • Is it secure? Hunting for hardcoded credentials, outdated encryption, and critical vulnerabilities hidden in legacy code.
  • What is the "Bus Factor"? Assessing reliance on undocumented tribal knowledge held by single developers.
